Rules :

  1. The game includes 2 players on one screen.
  2. Each player has a temporary score (ROUND) and a global score (GLOBAL).
  3. On each turn, the player has his ROUND set to 0 and can roll a die as many times as he wishes. a die as many times as he wishes.
  4. The result of a throw is added to the ROUND.
  5. During his turn, the player can decide at any time to:
    • Click on the "Hold" option, which allows you to send the points from the ROUND to the GLOBAL. It will then be the other player's turn.
    • Roll the die. If he rolls a 1, his ROUND score is lost and his turn is over.
  6. The first player to reach 100 points on global wins the game.
